All-in-One Open English Learning Platform developed by Hong Kong Metropolitan University and funded by Quality Education Fund (QEF) of the Hong Kong Government Education Bureau.
The learning platform aims to transform the existing Open English Textbook series into interactive, online and multimedia components, such as videos, animations, AI, app, platform. It provides an immersive English language learning experience via new features like gamified learning and AI Chatbot.
Unified Learning Platform
One-stop access to core textbook content and supplementary resources.

Multimedia components
Motivate learning with a mix of multimedia components (e.g. audio, animations, graphics)
Customizable Content
